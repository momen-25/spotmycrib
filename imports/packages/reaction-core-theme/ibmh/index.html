How it works

Watch the video
  • Explore projects

    iBidmyHome offers a range of projects across all major cities from Grade-A builders, at BELOW MARKET PRICE.

  • Choose Your Home

    Once you have identified a project it’s time to select YOUR HOME. To help you with your decision we provide all possible information about the property you have chosen.

  • Bid for your dream apartment

    Placing a Bid is simple. If you have to bid higher than the previous bidder, then just click on “Out Bid”. Outbidding will be by a pre-determined amount per sq foot You can place a bid on more than one unit by paying the “Auction Participation Fee” again.

  • Confirm your Home

    Once the Auction is closed and if your bid is the HIGHEST, you will be announced as a winning Bidder in the “My Bids” section. To confirm your selection, you need to pay the “Unit confirmation Deposit”, within 48 hrs. Failing which the “Second Highest Bidder” will be given an opportunity to choose that unit.
